ferequently asked question:

Is Seaweed considered a SuperFood?

yes! it is considered a superfood because of their high concentration of vitamins and minerals

Can Seaweed actually help me lose weight?

Seaweed helps you lose weight by delaying hunger. It contains a lot of fiber and no calories. that mixture may slow down stomach emptying, that helps you feel fuller for longer and can delay hunger pangs. It contains fucoxanthin (carotenoid) which contributes to an increased metabolism.

Can I add seaweed to my meals?

Seaweed is an increasingly popular ingredient in cuisines all over the world. Please check out some of our recommended recipes!

How can Seaweed improve my health?

Seaweed can help ensure you get the full range of amino acids - it is a good source of omega-3 fats and vitamin B12. It helps reduce your risk of type 2 diabetes by improving blood sugar control. It has a variety of protective antioxidants which can make unstable substances in your body, called free radicals, less reactive. Various research claim that it can help in cancer and tutor treatments.

What kind of vitamin and minerals does seaweed contains?

It’s the best dietary source of iodine, which helps support your thyroid gland. It also contains other vitamins and minerals, such as vitamin K, B vitamins, zinc and iron, along with antioxidants that help protect your cells from damage.”
